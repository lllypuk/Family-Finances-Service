Creating Effective Budgets

  • Be Realistic: Set amounts you can actually stick to
  • Start Small: Begin with one category (like groceries) before expanding
  • Review Regularly: Check your progress weekly to stay on track
  • Category vs All: Category budgets are more specific, "All Categories" gives you overall control
  • Period Length: Monthly budgets work well for most expenses, weekly for variable costs

Budget Periods

  • Weekly: Good for groceries, entertainment, gas
  • Monthly: Perfect for most recurring expenses
  • Yearly: Great for insurance, subscriptions, vacation funds
  • Custom: Use for specific projects or events
